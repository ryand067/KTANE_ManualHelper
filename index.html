Other Modules

Subject of Addition

Compute the sum on the display and enter it using the numbered buttons to solve the module. Use the grey "A" button to cycle through 10 numbers which will need added together. Use the red "C" button to clear your input, and the "+" button to submit it.

NOTE: Do NOT prepend "0"s to your number. There will ALWAYS be 10 numbers to add together.

Subject of 1,000 Words

The module contains 5 displays that spell out 5 letter words. Determine if the word shown is in the manual table. Press YES if the word is in the munual table. If not press NO. Answer five times in a row correctly to solve the module. You will get a strike if one or more of the answers you sent are incorrect.

For this manual helper, since the list is so long, the giant table have been replaced with a look-up function. Simply type the word in and recieve a "YES" or "NO" answer to relay to the diffuser.

WARNING: Strikes will not be recorded until after all five words are answered. Even if the first word is answered incorrectly.
